Uh-Oh ... You Found Me.

Designer by day, wannabe Viking by night!

I'm Kevin Keen. You can call me Keen! I'm a currently a Sophomore studing Global Business and Digital Arts with a minor in Computing at the University of Waterloo. I am a Designer who can mediate between the jargon of code and the language of business.

Being a Canadian citizen, I have travelled to 34 States with 31 of them being in a RV. I almost enlisted in the Canadian Armed Forces to study to become an Infantry Men, but my aptitude test said otherwise. The results from the test centered around creative thinking, communication, and problem solving. Coming from an arts highschool, I continued to pursue a higher level of education instead of joining the military.

In my free time, I enjoy playing basketball, watching Anime, listening to podcasts, and getting active with my Labradoodle. I'm constantly learning more about how Design and Development play a huge role in creating the future Designers.
